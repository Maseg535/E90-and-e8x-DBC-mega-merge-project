0x0A8 (DME): Engine torque, brake and clutch status. TORQ_AVL (B2 signed, repeated in B4) is torque demand: ~0x02 at idle, +0x2E (~47) heavy acceleration, 0xFA–0xFD (~−3 to −6) during engine braking/overrun fuel cut. HIL-confirmed in 5min_run_for_testing.log 2026-03-26: highest values (0x27–0x2F) during sustained acceleration in 6th gear.
